Developers

Shortly after 1.1.0 release, Arne Vandamme joined me to become a second member of the development team. I am still responsible for the overall concept of JRobin, core coding (org.jrobin.core.* package) and JRobin add-ons (MRTG-demo application, RRD inspector, etc). Arne dedicated himself to the graphing portion of the package (org.jrobin.graph.*) - he did a great work by rewriting this part of JRobin from the scratch.
